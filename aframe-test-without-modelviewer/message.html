This is an interactive 3D model of the LFPO molecule.

Controls:

  • Desktop: Mouse drag (rotate), Mouse wheel (zoom)
  • Mobile: Swipe (rotate), Pinch (zoom)
  • VR: Click and drag (rotate), Thumbstick (zoom)
  • AR: Point and use trigger button (reposition), Thumbstick (zoom)

AR mode relies on the WebXR Augmented Reality Module proposal. Browser support is experimental and might vary across vendors.
